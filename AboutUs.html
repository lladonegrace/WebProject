About Us

Welcome to MARCA, the Modern Agriculture and Rice Cultivation Authority. We are at the forefront of transforming traditional rice farming in the Philippines by embracing and promoting modern harvesting practices. Our mission is to provide comprehensive insights into the impact of these practices on socio-economic development, crop yield, and technological advancements within the agricultural sector.

At MARCA, our team of dedicated experts includes seasoned agronomists, environmentalists, and technology enthusiasts. Together, we work relentlessly to bridge the gap between traditional farming methods and innovative, sustainable practices. Our commitment extends beyond information sharing; we actively collaborate with farmers, researchers, and policymakers to drive positive change in the agricultural landscape.

Join us on this journey towards a more efficient, sustainable, and technologically-advanced future for Philippine agriculture.
